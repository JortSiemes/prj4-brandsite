What is REFACT and what is our Mission

"In an online world filled with lies and disinformation we believe that people have the right to know if something is true or not because only with quality information you can create an authentic opinion." Nowadays,there is so much information and different sources on the Internet. With so much information you can never be sure what is actually true. That is why we "REFACT" exist. We want to help You find the right information you need. Refact offers a safe place where you can check your sources and their information in order to make sure it is exactly what you need. We want to exceed to your expectation with reliability, respect, innovation and knowledge.
